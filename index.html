The Mexican food Cooking Club

We are a cooking club based in The Netherlands. We love Mexican food. This club is all about the delicious food from Mexico and the southern states of the US.We come toghether twice a month to cook toghether and share our recipes.

Emily's Green Taco's

Emily made taco's with mostly green veggies. These soft taco's are easy to make and great to eat on Taco Teusday! Checkout her recipe! Taco's are a nice meal, because you can switch out the vegetables, with veggies, you like. Emily, made hers, width avocado and green bell peppers.
